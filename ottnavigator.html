How to Add Live TV M3U URL in OTT Navigator (Complete Guide)

OTT Navigator is a powerful IPTV player for Android and Android TV. This guide explains how to add a Live TV M3U playlist URL using a step-by-step method with premium UI screenshots.

Requirements:
  • OTT Navigator installed (Free or Premium)
  • TNM3U Live TV M3U URL
  • Stable internet connection (3.4-5.5 Mbps)

Step 1: Open OTT Navigator App

Launch the OTT Navigator app on your Android phone or Android TV. You will see the main dashboard screen.

Step 2: Open Settings Menu

From the home screen, click on the Settings (⚙️) icon. Then select Provider.

Step 3: Add New Playlist

Tap on Add Playlist and choose Playlist (URL / File).

Step 4: Enter Live TV M3U URL

Paste your Live TV M3U URL and give your playlist a name.

http://tnm3u.live/tv.m3u
  • Playlist Name: TNM3U Live TV
  • Click Apply / OK
Premium Tips:
  • Enable “Start last channel on app launch”
  • Use Hardware Decoder for smooth playback
  • Download App is Premium version contains no ads and improves speed

Common Issues & Fixes

  • Channels not loading: Check M3U URL and refresh playlist
  • EPG not showing: Add correct XMLTV URL
  • Buffering: Try different decoder settings

Conclusion

By following this guide, you can easily add Live TV channels to OTT Navigator using an M3U playlist. This method works on Android phones, Android TV, and Fire TV devices.
